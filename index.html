Tuesday, 10 June — Portugal Day 🇵🇹
Alfama, Castle & Belém
  • 8:30 — Early start! Head to São Jorge Castle — explore the battlements and enjoy Lisbon's best panoramic views (€10 entry). Go early to beat the crowds
  • 10:00 — Wind down through Alfama's medieval lanes — azulejo tiles, street art, laundry-strung alleys, hidden courtyards. Sé Cathedral exterior
  • 11:30 — Ride Tram 28 through historic neighbourhoods to Estrela or Chiado
  • 12:30 — Lunch at Taberna da Rua das Flores (book ahead!) or Discreto in Alfama
  • 14:00 — Tram 15E or Uber to Belém. First stop: Pastéis de Belém — the original custard tarts since 1837. Eat warm.
  • 14:45 — Jerónimos Monastery exterior — stunning UNESCO Manueline architecture. Admire from the outside (confirm holiday hours)
  • 15:30 — Walk to Tower of Belém — iconic riverside fortress. Beautiful from outside (skip the queue inside)
  • 16:00 — Walk along the Belém waterfront — stunning riverside promenade with views of the 25 de Abril bridge and Cristo Rei
  • 17:00 — LX Factory — creative hub in a converted industrial complex. Independent shops, Ler Devagar bookshop, street art
  • 19:00 — Farewell-worthy dinner at Belcanto (2 Michelin stars, €265 tasting — ⚠️ reservation sent) or BouBou's (7-course from €80)
  • 🔄 Walk-in backup: Time Out Market (daily 10:00–00:00, €8–15/dish) · Canalha in Belém (Tue–Sat 12:00–23:00, €30–45pp)
🇵🇹 10 June is Dia de Portugal — national holiday. Festive atmosphere, some altered hours. Restaurants stay open.

Belém → LX Factory: Uber 5 min, €4. Or walk 15 min along Rua de Belém.

Wednesday, 11 June
Sintra — Fairy-Tale Palaces
  • 8:00 — Early train from Rossio Station to Sintra (~40 min, €4.90 return). Sit on the left for best views
  • 9:00 — Bus 434 or Uber to Pena Palace — colourful Romanticist hilltop castle. Book timed tickets online NOW (€14 entry, sells out in June)

  • 14:00 — Quinta da Regaleira — Gothic mansion, mystical gardens, the famous Initiation Well (€10)

  • 18:00 — Train back to Lisbon
  • 19:00 — Seafood feast at Cervejaria Ramiro (Tue–Sun 12:00–23:00, closed Mon; no reservation, expect 30–45 min queue) — giant prawns, percebes, and the legendary steak sandwich finale
  • 🔄 Walk-in backup: Prado (Tue–Sat 12:30–15:00 & 19:00–23:00, €35–50pp) · Cervejaria Ramiro queue usually moves fast — worth the wait
⚠️ Book Pena Palace timed tickets at parfruessintra.pt right now. June slots sell out days in advance.
🗺️ Step-by-Step: Hotel → Sintra
1. Hotel → Rossio Station: Walk to Praça do Rossio — the station entrance is the ornate horseshoe-arch building on the northwest side of the square (2–5 min from your hotel). Look for the Neo-Manueline façade.
2. Buy ticket: At the ticket machines inside. Return ticket to Sintra = €4.90. Use your Viva Viagem card or buy a paper ticket.
3. Board train: CP train to Sintra (every 20 min). 40 min ride. Sit on the LEFT side for best views.
4. Sintra Station → Pena Palace: Exit station, cross the road. Bus 434 stop is right outside (€7 hop-on-hop-off, runs every 15–20 min, 15 min ride up the hill). OR Uber ~€8.

Return: Train back from Sintra station to Rossio (same platform, same 40 min). Last trains run until ~midnight.

🚇 Viva Viagem Card

Buy at any metro station (€0.50 for the card). Load credit and tap for metro, buses, and trams. Also works on Sintra & Cascais trains.

🚗 Why No Rental Car?

Lisbon's hills and narrow lanes make driving stressful. Parking is scarce and expensive. Uber is €4–8 across the city. Train to Sintra & Cascais is fast and scenic.

Before You Go
Practical Tips

☀️ Weather (June)

Warm and sunny. 27–30°C highs, 16–18°C evenings. Very little rain. Sintra can be foggy mornings. Pack sunscreen, light layers, comfy walking shoes.

💶 Money

Euro (€). 1 EUR ≈ HK$8.5–8.7. Cards accepted almost everywhere. Carry ~€50 cash for small tascas and kiosks. Tipping 5–10% is generous.

📱 Connectivity

Get an Airalo eSIM before departure — works great in Portugal, no roaming stress. Free Wi-Fi in most hotels and cafés.

🔒 Safety

Lisbon is very safe. Watch for pickpockets on Tram 28 and in Baixa/Alfama tourist zones. Uber/Bolt for late-night transport.

🗣️ Language

English widely spoken in tourist areas. Key phrases: Obrigado/a (thank you), Bom dia (good morning), Por favor (please).

📋 Book NOW

1) Pena Palace timed tickets. 2) Belcanto/Alma dinner. 3) Hotel. June is peak season — don't wait.
